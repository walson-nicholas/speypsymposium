Lester Clark

Group COO, Oilserv Limited

Area of Focus: Energy Infrastructure: Gap and Potential

Lester Clark is currently Group Chief Operating Officer of Oilserv Group. Prior to joining Oilserv, Lester served in many Senior Leadership positions including Deputy Chief Technical Officer at IHS Towers and Group Chief Operating Officer at AOS Orwell. Lester has been living and working in Nigeria since 2014 having moved following a 17 year career with Baker Hughes Ltd where he held the position of Sub Sahara Operations Director, Russia Caspian Product Line Director, Marketing Director and other Product Management, Technical and Sales roles. Lester is passionate about People Development, HSSE, Leadership, Continuous Performance Improvement and Delighting Customers.
